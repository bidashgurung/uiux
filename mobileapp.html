About Project


Problem Statement

Flim Fuse is designed to cater to the needs of movie enthusiasts, FlimFuse brings the magic of cinema right to your fingertips, whether you're a student looking for a break from campus life or a busy professional seeking relaxation after a hectic day.With FlimFuse, you can browse, book, and enjoy your favorite movies with just a few taps on your device.


Project Duration

Feburary 2024 to May 2024.


The Problem

Lack of online platform for booking move ticket


The Goal

To design an online mobile application that allows users to book the movie ticket from anywhere and anytime.

My role as Designer


As a UX designer spearheading the creation of FlimFuse, a revolutionary movie booking app, my journey from inception to launch is marked by a meticulous focus on delivering an unparalleled user experience.


My responsibility as Designer


Conducting interviews, paper and digital wireframing, low and high-fidelity prototyping, conducting usability studies, accounting for accessibility, and iterating on designs.





User Research: Pain Points

Limited Information

The limited access to information about upcoming movies, showtimes, and savailable seats

Waiting in queue

Have to wait in queue which is time consuming for the customers to arrive their turn.

Risk of sold out Screening.

Popular movies may sell out quickly, leaving some moviegoers disappointed and unable to attend their desired showtimes.

User ResearchStudy


User research is a crucial aspect of the design and development process for any product or service. Conducting a user research study provides valuable insights and helps ensure that the final product meets the needs and expectations of the target audience.



User Research


User research is essential for understanding user needs, behaviors, and pain points, ensuring that products are designed to be user-friendly and meet real-world requirements. It helps identify and fix usability issues early, supports informed design decisions, and enhances overall user satisfaction and engagement.



Mock Up


Mockups are essential in the design process because they provide a detailed visual representation of the final product, allowing stakeholders to see and understand the design before development begins.They help in validating design choices, facilitating feedback, and ensuring that the final product aligns with the project's vision and user expectations.

High Fidelity


High-fidelity (hi-fi) prototypes are crucial because they closely resemble the final product in both appearance and functionality, allowing for more accurate testing and feedback. They help stakeholders and users understand the design in a realistic context, facilitate better usability testing, and ensure that design issues are identified and addressed before full-scale development begins.




Competitive Audit


Conducting a competitor audit is essential because it provides valuable insights into the strengths and weaknesses of competing products or services, helping to identify market gaps and opportunities. This process informs strategic decisions, allowing you to differentiate your offering, improve upon competitors' shortcomings, and ensure your product meets or exceeds industry standards and user expectations.

Competitive Audit Report


A competitive report is crucial as it offers a comprehensive analysis of the market landscape, highlighting the strengths, weaknesses, strategies, and performance of competitors. This information helps businesses make informed decisions, identify opportunities for differentiation, improve their own strategies, and gain a competitive edge by understanding industry trends and anticipating market shifts.

Impact

The app's design enhanced the movie ticket booking experience, making it more accessible and user-friendly, with an intuitive layout and clear navigation, making it quick and hassle-free.

What I Learned

This project taught me user-centered design principles, iterative testing, user feedback, aesthetic appeal, functionality, and accessibility. It highlighted the importance of continuous improvement and adaptation based on user needs and preferences.
